Trig Funcs and Hypo Trig Funcs

发表于 2024-12-10 更新于 2025-01-05

Section 0 \[ \begin{aligned} &\sinh x=\frac{e^{x}-e^{-x}}{2}\\ &\cosh x=\frac{e^{x}+e^{-x}}{2}\\ &\operatorname{arsinh} x=\ln(x+\sqrt{ x^{2}+1 })\\ &\operatorname{arcosh} x=\ln(x+\sqrt{ x^{2}-1 }) \end{aligned} \] 三角函数、反三角函数、双曲函数、反双曲函数的图像、单调性、奇偶性、特殊值。

Section 1 \[ \begin{aligned} &\sin^{2} x+\cos^{2}x=1\\ &\sec^{2}x-\tan^{2}x=1\\ &\csc^{2}x-\cot^{2}x=1 \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} &\cosh^{2}x-\sinh^{2}x=1\\ &\tanh^{2}x+\operatorname{sech}^{2}x=1\\ &\operatorname{coth}^{2}x+\operatorname{csch}^{2}x=1 \end{aligned} \]

Section 2

\[ \begin{aligned} &\sin(x\pm y)=\sin x\cos y\pm \cos x\sin y\\ &\cos(x\pm y)=\cos x\cos y\mp \sin x\sin y\\ &\tan (x\pm y)=\frac{\tan x \pm \tan y}{1\mp\tan x\tan y} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} &\sinh(x\pm y)=\sin x\cos y\pm \cos x\sin y\\ &\cosh(x\pm y)=\cos x\cos y {\color{red}\pm} \sin x\sin y\\ &\tanh (x\pm y)=\frac{\tan x \pm \tan y}{1{\color{red}\pm}\tan x\tan y} \end{aligned} \]

Section 3

\[ \begin{aligned} &\sin 2x=2\sin x\cos x\\ &\cos 2x=\cos^{2}x-\sin^{2}x=2\cos^{2}x-1=1-2\sin^{2}x\\ &\tan 2x=\frac{2\tan x}{1-\tan^{2} x}\\ &\sin^{2}x=\frac{1-\cos 2x}{2}\\ &\cos^{2}x=\frac{1+\cos 2x}{2} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \begin{aligned} &\sinh 2x=2\sinh x\cosh x\\ &\cosh 2x=\cosh^{2}x{\color{red}+}\sinh^{2}x=2\cosh^{2}x-1=1{\color{red}+}2\sinh^{2}x\\ &\tanh 2x=\frac{2\tanh x}{1{\color{red}+}\tanh^{2} x}\\ &\sinh^{2}x={\color{red}-}\frac{1-\cosh 2x}{2}\\ &\cosh^{2}x=\frac{1+\cosh 2x}{2} \end{aligned} \]

Section 4

\[ \begin{aligned} &(\sin x)'=\cos x\\ &(\cos x)'=-\sin x\\ &(\tan x)'=\sec^{2}x\\ &(\cot x)'=-\csc^{2}x\\ &(\sec x)'=\sec x\tan x\\ &(\csc x)'=-\csc x\cot x\\ \end{aligned}\qquad \begin{aligned} &(\sinh x)'=\cosh x\\ &(\cosh x)'=\sinh x\\ &(\tanh x)'=\operatorname{sech}^{2}x\\ &(\coth x)'=-\operatorname{csch}^{2}x\\ &(\operatorname{sech} x)'=-\operatorname{sech} x\tanh x\\ &(\operatorname{csch} x)'=-\operatorname{csch} x\coth x\\ \end{aligned} \]

Section 5

\[ \begin{aligned} &\int \sin x=-\cos x+C\\ &\int \cos x=\sin x+C\\ \end{aligned}\qquad \begin{aligned} &\int \sinh x=\cosh x+C\\ &\int \cosh x=\sinh x+C\\ \end{aligned} \]

Section 6

\[ \begin{aligned} &(\arcsin x)'=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1-x^{2} }}\\ &(\arccos x)'=-\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1-x^{2} }}\\ &(\arctan x)'=\frac{1}{1+x^{2}}\\ \end{aligned}\qquad \begin{aligned} &(\operatorname{arsinh} x)'=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ 1+x^{2} }}\\ &(\operatorname{arcosh} x)'=\frac{1}{\sqrt{ x^{2}-1 }}\\ \end{aligned} \]
